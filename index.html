Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Delivery

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to over 100 countries worldwide, with extensive coverage in major cities and regions.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on the service selected. Local deliveries typically take 1-2 days, while international shipments may take 3-7 business days.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for eligible locations and packages. Contact us for availability in your area.

Pricing & Services

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are based on package weight, dimensions, destination, and selected service level.

Do you offer bulk shipping discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for bulk shipments and regular customers. Contact our sales team for custom quotes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and mobile money payments.
